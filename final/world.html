Clan De Luna

Hidden in the mountains of the Vale lives the elusive Clan de Luna.

Mysterious... Hauntingly Beautiful... Closed off... Cautious.

They honor the dance of the moonlight rays across the surface.
Dreams are one's personal prophecy from the Fates.

If you somehow find their home, don't expect to remember the way back.
