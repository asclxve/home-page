The Fates once said...

In the land of Eldesteele
Lies a war of nature and machine...

Where one side fills the world
With promises of immortality and grandeur
Simultaneously polluting nature with obessed,
greedy ideologies...

A never ending cycle of corruption and darkness.

Where another side is tethered to the lands of the Mother Vale...
For they believe in the cycles of life and the word of the Fates

Believe in the fact that nature will provide all, not the ideas of man.

A strict belief unable to be forced out of their paths in fact. Or so they believe...
