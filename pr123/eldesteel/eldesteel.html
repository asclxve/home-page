❉ELDESTEEL❉

The Mood Board

Eldesteel is my newest worldbuilding project. This one came as a combination of a new character design plot, making a new D&D character, and tossing in inspiration from Arcane (because the brainrot is BAD)

The theme of this one is a steampunk/fantasy nature esque world, where machinery and nature co-exist and can survive with one another (like Horizon Zero Dawn), with fantasy magic elements of D&D and dystopian steampunk of Arcane.

SKETCHES

This is the work in progress part: the character I'm making for this world. This isn't the final design, but it is the only one I sorta finished.

Their name is Soliel and they are a Forest Druid that becomes a warforged elf after a bad accident left them for dead. They loathe all things machines, and they have a partner who is an artificer and responsible for quite literally piecing them back together and bringing them to life.
